About ElimuTrack

Empowering Kinari Primary School with Digital Excellence.

Our Mission

To provide a seamless, efficient, and transparent digital platform that enhances the educational experience for teachers, parents, and students at Kinari Primary School.

ElimuTrack bridges the gap between home and school, ensuring that every parent is informed, every fee is accounted for, and every student's progress is tracked accurately under the new CBC curriculum.

Our History

Founded in 2025, ElimuTrack was developed to solve the challenges of manual record-keeping and delayed communication.

Innovation

We leverage modern technology like M-Pesa integration and SMS APIs to keep our school running smoothly.

Community

Built specifically for the Kinari community, ensuring that our local education standards are world-class.
